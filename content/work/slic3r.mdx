A fork of the open-source slic3r 3D printing slicer. Modified the slicing algorithm and export pipeline to support custom print workflows not available in the upstream release.

The project served as a deep-dive into computational geometry and the OpenGL rendering pipeline — a different problem domain that sharpened my reading of unfamiliar codebases.
